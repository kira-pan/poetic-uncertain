IMAGE-BASED ENGLISH PHRASE STRUCTURE RULES
(root folder: word_cutouts/)


GOAL
Generate sentence-like image sequences that follow English phrase structure.
Meaning, capitalization, agreement, and tense do not matter.


AVAILABLE FOLDERS
word_cutouts/
  determiner/
  adjective/
  noun/
  verb/
  adverb/
  preposition/
  NP/
  VP/
  prepositional_phrase/


--------------------------------
SENTENCE RULES (S)
--------------------------------


S → NP VP
S → NP VP NP
S → NP VP PP
S → PP NP VP
S → NP VP NP PP
S → NP VP PP PP


Choose one S rule per generation.


--------------------------------
NOUN PHRASE RULES (NP)
--------------------------------


NP may be selected from:
- word_cutouts/NP/


OR constructed using:


NP → Det N
NP → Det Adj N
NP → Det Adj Adj N
NP → Adj N
NP → N
NP → N PP


Constraints:
- Determiner optional
- Max 2 adjectives
- Max 1 PP per NP


--------------------------------
VERB PHRASE RULES (VP)
--------------------------------


VP may be selected from:
- word_cutouts/VP/


OR constructed using:


VP → V
VP → V NP
VP → Adv V
VP → V Adv
VP → Adv V NP
VP → V NP Adv
VP → V PP
VP → Adv V NP PP


Constraints:
- Adverb optional
- NP optional
- Max 2 adverbs
- Max 1 PP per VP


--------------------------------
PREPOSITIONAL PHRASE RULES (PP)
--------------------------------


PP may be selected from:
- word_cutouts/prepositional_phrase/


OR constructed using:


PP → Prep NP


--------------------------------
ATTACHMENT LIMITS
--------------------------------


- Max 2 total PPs per sentence
- Avoid more than 2 modifiers in sequence
- Hard cap sentence length ~15–18 image tokens


--------------------------------
RANDOMNESS GUIDELINES
--------------------------------


Suggested probabilities:
- use prebuilt NP: 25%
- use prebuilt VP: 20%
- use prebuilt PP: 20%
- add adjective to NP: 60%
- add adverb to VP: 50%
- add PP to NP or VP: 30%


--------------------------------
END OF RULES
--------------------------------
